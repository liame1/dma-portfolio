ARTIST STATEMENT // PURPOSE:

“Liam is an artist that simply must create, he is also confused why he is writing about himself in the third-person. Anyway... Every day, I wake up inspired by the colors of the sky, the sounds of the universe, and the infinite possibilities of the human spirit. My art is a motivated by my soul, dreams, and caffiene. I love watching people connect with my work, it’s like a cosmic collaboration between my imagination and their metaphorical-metaphysical perception of the universe. I wish to continue learning new skills that will broaden my potential of what I can create, and further my goals. My current goal is to learn how to properly use the ctrl+s shortcut before my program crashes. I would also like to learn how to make pizza.
